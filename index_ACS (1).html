Getting Started

Logging in: pick your name from the dropdown, choose your shift type (Opening or Closing) and side (Well or Quarantine), then enter your PIN. Default PIN is 1234 unless you've set your own — leadership can update PINs in Settings.

Leadership: you can skip the shift/side picker and log straight in. Once inside, use the "Viewing" card at the top of the Dashboard to switch which side/shift's checklist you're looking at.

Dashboard Tab

Shift Updates: anything the team needs to know right now, marked either 🔴 Important or 🔵 Info. Tap "Mark Read" once you've seen one.

Reminders: ongoing behavior or medical notes for specific dogs that the whole team should keep in mind. Resolve one once it no longer applies.

Checklist Tab

Shows the tasks for your side and shift. Check off tasks as you go, everyone can see progress in real time. You can add your own task with the "+ Add" box at the bottom if something comes up that's not already on the list.

Leadership can reorder, remove, or add tasks permanently for everyone in Settings → Edit Shift Checklists.

Passdown Tab

Fill this out before your shift ends so the next shift knows what's going on in each kennel. For each kennel: dog name, whether it's been deep cleaned, poop levels, and eating amounts.

Mom Ate / Puppies Ate: these are separate on purpose. If it's just a mom with no litter, or a litter with no mom present, just leave the field that doesn't apply blank.

Use the history section to look back through the last 7 days of passdowns, so you can always check how a previous shift went instead of relying on memory or old texts. Leadership can also pull the full passdown history anytime from Reports → Export Passdown CSV, since nothing is ever deleted from the Sheet.

Reports & Settings (Leadership)

Reports: completion stats, trends over time, and a full exportable log you can filter by date range.

Settings: add or deactivate staff, reset PINs, and edit checklists for either side/shift. Changes here apply for the whole team immediately, no file edits or GitHub needed.

Something Not Working?

If the dashboard seems stuck or won't save, try refreshing the page first. If a specific action fails repeatedly, let leadership know what you were doing when it happened so it can get sorted out.
