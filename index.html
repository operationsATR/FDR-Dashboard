Getting Started

Logging in: pick your name from the dropdown and enter your PIN. Default PIN is 1234 unless you've set your own — leadership can update PINs in Settings.

Dashboard Tab

Shift Updates: anything the team needs to know right now, marked either 🔴 Important or 🔵 Info. Tap "Mark Read" once you've seen one.

Dogs — Requirements at a Glance: shows each dog's tier/stop color and requirements at a glance, so you're not stuck trying to remember who needs what before an appointment or adoption.

Today's Appointments: pulls straight from Acuity automatically, showing time, dog, adopter, and handler for the day. Tap ↻ Refresh if it looks out of date, or "Open Acuity" to see full appointment details.

Checklist Tab

Daily, Weekly, and Monthly tabs, each with tasks pulled from our SOPs. Check tasks off as you go, everyone can see progress in real time. Add your own task with the "+ Add" box if something comes up that's not already listed.

Reports & Settings (Leadership)

Reports: completion stats, trends over time, and a full exportable log you can filter by date range.

Settings: add or deactivate staff, reset PINs, and update the Acuity feed URL if it ever needs to change. Changes here apply for the whole team immediately.

Something Not Working?

If the dashboard seems stuck or won't save, try refreshing the page first. If appointments aren't showing up, try the ↻ Refresh button before assuming something's broken. If a specific action fails repeatedly, let leadership know what you were doing when it happened.
